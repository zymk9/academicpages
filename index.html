Research

I'm interested in 3D vision, differentiable rendering, real-time rendering, generative AI, as well as other computer vision and graphics fields. My past research is mainly on empowering radiance fields and other 3D representations with the capability of scene understanding. I also work on uncertainty quantification in differentiable rendering, and accelerating rendering with temporal reprojection.
